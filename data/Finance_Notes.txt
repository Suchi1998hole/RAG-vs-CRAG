Finance Fundamentals - 100 Line Notes
=============================================================
1. Finance is the study of managing money and investments.
2. It focuses on how individuals, firms, and governments raise and use funds.
3. Main branches: personal finance, corporate finance, public finance.
4. Assets are resources owned that provide future benefits.
5. Liabilities are obligations owed to others.
6. Equity represents ownership in a company.
7. Balance Sheet shows assets, liabilities, and equity.
8. Income Statement shows revenues and expenses.
9. Cash Flow Statement tracks inflows and outflows of cash.
10. Profit = Revenue - Expenses.

11. Time Value of Money (TVM): money today is worth more than tomorrow.
12. Present Value (PV) discounts future cash flows.
13. Future Value (FV) compounds current value over time.
14. Compound Interest grows exponentially.
15. Simple Interest grows linearly.
16. Risk and return are directly related.
17. Diversification reduces portfolio risk.
18. Stocks represent ownership; bonds represent debt.
19. Mutual funds pool money from many investors.
20. ETFs are exchange-traded versions of mutual funds.
